### broadband  /ˈbrɔːd.bænd/

 - **中文**: 寬頻
 - **詞性**: n.
 - **搭配詞**: 
    - **broadband connection (寬頻連接)** 
    - **broadband internet (寬頻網際網路)**
- **例句**: 
    - **The broadband connection allows for faster internet speeds. (寬頻連接允許更快的網速。)**
