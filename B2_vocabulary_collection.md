### 重點單字

anxiety /æŋˈzaɪəti/ - 焦慮、擔憂 (noun)
- 📝 *She felt anxiety before the important interview. (她在重要面試前感到焦慮。)*

authentic /ɔːˈθentɪk/ - 真實的、正宗的 (adjective)
- 📝 *The restaurant serves authentic Italian food. (這家餐廳提供正宗的義大利菜。)*
